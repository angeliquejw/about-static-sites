Static Site Generators

Pros

  • Zippy
    No DB queries!
  • Low maintenance, easy to back up
  • Source control
    Files AND content
  • More secure
  • Low-cost or free hosting
  • Very personalized
    Use your favorite markup language, favorite text editor

Cons

  • No built-in comments
  • No out-of-the-box search
  • Tied to your local dev
    No updates from your phone
  • No scheduled posts
  • No GUI
    Be clear about who will be using, updating site
  • Paralyzed by choice
